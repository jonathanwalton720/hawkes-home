Our Mission

The success of these men at Hawkes Home translates to a better world for all!

Non-Profit

Hawkes Home, Inc., a 501(c)3 non-profit corporation was formed to provide temporary housing and reentry education for men exiting prison or jail. As our clients strive to reclaim their dignity and rebuild their lives, they face the daunting challenge of adjusting to the changes in society that took place during their confinement.

Participation

Through participation in Narcotics Anonymous (NA) and Alcoholics Anonymous (AA) and by using their newly-acquired life skills, clients will be encouraged to avoid old contacts and the destructive behaviors that originally led to their incarceration.

Business of Living

Clients will have opportunities to learn the practical skills of the business of living. Skills such as job searching, money management, computer proficiency, health maintenance, independent living, and other abilities are explored to help launch new lives for themselves on the outside as productive members of society.

 

Get Involved

Fresh Start

We at Hawkes Home, in order to provide the clients with a fresh start, offer a clean and sober place to live at no charge for the first 2 months. We also provide them with a bus pass, clothing, hygiene articles and other items they may need.

For us at Hawkes Home to be able to provide for the clients as well as keep up the maintenance of the home, we look to the generosity of people and businesses in our community for charitable donations.

If you have anything that you feel could be useful, please contact us here at Hawkes Home.
