MISSION STATEMENT

HAWKES HOME, Inc., a 501(c)3 non-profit corporation was formed to provide temporary housing and reentry education for men exiting prison or jail. As they strive to reclaim their dignity and rebuild their lives, our clients face the daunting challenge of adjusting to the changes in society that took place during their confinement.

Through participation in Narcotics Anonymous (NA) and Alcoholics Anonymous (AA) and by using their newly acquired life skills, the men will be encouraged to avoid old contacts and the destructive behaviors that originally led to the trouble that resulted in their incarceration.

They will have opportunities to learn the practical skills of the "business of living," i.e. job search, money management, computer proficiency, health maintenance, independent living, and other abilities required to launch satisfying new lives for themselves on the outside as productive members of society.

The success of these men at the Hawkes House translates to a better world for all!
